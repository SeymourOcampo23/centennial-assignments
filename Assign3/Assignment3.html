BMW M3 E30

The BMW M3 E30 is a legendary symbol of automotive excellence, renowned for its track dominance, agile handling, and iconic design. Born from BMW's racing heritage, its lightweight construction and high-revving engine ensured unparalleled performance on the road and track. With limited production numbers adding to its allure, the M3 E30 continues to captivate enthusiasts as a timeless classic, embodying the pinnacle of driving exhilaration and precision engineering.

Engine & Performance

  • Torque: 170lb-ft
